Our Plumbing Services

At Superior Plumbing Solutions LLC, our licensed professionals provide dependable, high-quality plumbing services across Charleston and the Tri-County area. Whether you need a repair, replacement, or installation, we focus on delivering lasting results with honest pricing and clear communication.

Water Heater Services

Tank & Tankless Water Heater Installation: Whether you're upgrading or replacing a unit, we install both traditional and tankless water heaters safely and efficiently.

Water Heater Repair & Maintenance: Consistent hot water matters. We troubleshoot and repair issues quickly, and offer maintenance to keep your system running smoothly.

Drain & Sewer Services

Drain Cleaning: Clear blockages and restore flow with professional-grade tools that eliminate clogs without damaging your pipes.

Sewer Camera Inspection: Our non-invasive camera inspections let us identify root problems in your sewer line fast and accurately.

Sewer Line Repair & Replacement: We repair damaged lines or replace failing pipes to protect your home and prevent costly backups.

Fixture Repair & Replacement

Faucets, Toilets & Sinks: From drips to full replacements, we handle leaky, outdated, or inefficient fixtures with reliable, water-saving options.

Showers & Tubs: Whether you’re remodeling or fixing low pressure or leaks, we deliver clean installs and lasting repairs.

Leak Detection & Repair

Leaks can waste water and cause major home damage if left unchecked. Our team uses advanced methods to detect and fix leaks quickly and cleanly.

Water Filtration & RO Systems

Enjoy clean, fresh water straight from the tap. We install and maintain whole-home filtration systems and under-sink reverse osmosis units to improve water quality and taste.
