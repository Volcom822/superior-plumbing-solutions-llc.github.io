About Superior Plumbing Solutions LLC

Welcome to Superior Plumbing Solutions LLC — where dependable service meets exceptional quality. Based here in the Charleston area of South Carolina, we’re a small, family-run business that prides itself on treating every customer like a neighbor, not just a number.

After almost 15 years in the trade, our founder started this company to offer something different: skilled plumbing by experienced professionals, without the sales pitch. We’ve seen the upcharges, gimmicks, and high-pressure quotes — and we wanted no part of it.

Whether you’re dealing with a clogged drain, a failing water heater, or planning a full plumbing remodel, we’ve got the tools and experience to do it right. We specialize in residential plumbing, water heater installs (tank & tankless), fixture replacement, whole-home water filtration systems, and RO systems.

We’re fully licensed, bonded, and insured in South Carolina, and proudly serve Charleston, Mt. Pleasant, Summerville, Goose Creek, North Charleston, and the surrounding Lowcountry.

Thanks for considering us — we look forward to earning your trust one job at a time.
