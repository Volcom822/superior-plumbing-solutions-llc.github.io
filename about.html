About Superior Plumbing Solutions LLC

Welcome to Superior Plumbing Solutions LLC — where dependable service meets exceptional quality. Based right here in Charleston, South Carolina, we’re a small, family-run business that prides itself on treating every customer like a neighbor, not just a number.

My name’s Chris, and I founded this company with one goal in mind: provide honest, high-quality plumbing services without the sales pitch. I’ve worked in this industry for years, and I’ve seen the good, the bad, and the overpriced. I wanted to do it differently — fair prices, real communication, and top-tier workmanship.

Whether you’re dealing with a clogged drain, a failing water heater, or planning a full plumbing system for your home build, we’ve got the tools and the experience to handle it all. We specialize in residential plumbing, water heater installations (tank & tankless), fixture replacements, and full plumbing remodels.

We’re fully licensed and insured in South Carolina, and proudly serve Charleston, Mt. Pleasant, Summerville, Goose Creek, North Charleston, and surrounding areas.

Thanks for considering us. We look forward to earning your trust one job at a time.
