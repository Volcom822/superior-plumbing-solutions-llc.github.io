About Superior Plumbing Solutions LLC

Welcome to Superior Plumbing Solutions LLC — where dependable service meets exceptional quality. We’re a small, family-run business proudly serving South Carolina’s Lowcountry. We take pride in leaving your home better than we found it — every time.

After nearly 15 years in the trade, our owner Chris saw how often homeowners were dealing with salespeople instead of real plumbers. Superior Plumbing Solutions was built to change that. We focus on what matters: quality work, fair pricing, and clear communication — no gimmicks, no pressure, just honest plumbing done right.

Whether you’re dealing with a clogged drain, a failing water heater, or planning a full plumbing system for your home build, we’ve got the tools and the experience to handle it all. We specialize in residential plumbing, water heater installations (tank & tankless), fixture replacements, and full plumbing remodels.

We’re licensed, bonded, and insured in South Carolina, proudly serving Charleston and the entire Tri-County area.

We appreciate the opportunity to earn your business. With every job, we’re committed to delivering the kind of service you’d recommend to family and friends.
