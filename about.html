About Superior Plumbing Solutions LLC

Welcome to Superior Plumbing Solutions LLC — where dependable service is backed by years of hands-on experience.

Based in the Charleston area of South Carolina, we’re a small, family-owned and operated business focused on delivering honest work.

After nearly 15 years in the plumbing trade, our founder Chris started Superior Plumbing Solutions with a simple goal: provide skilled, professional service — without the sales pitch.

We’re here to offer straightforward solutions, clear communication, and top-tier workmanship for homeowners across the Charleston Tri-County area and surrounding communities.

Whether it’s a water heater install, unclogging a drain, fixture upgrade, or a full home repipe, we take pride in getting the job done right — the first time.
